Realtor & Home Seller Staging

Stage homes on the outside to sell!

First impressions count. A lack of curb appeal can be deadly in today's real-estate market.

Staging homes has become a standard practice for selling a property at the best price. Staging for curb appeal is no exception. A beautiful front garden, lush and weed-free lawn, and colorful window boxes set the stage for home buyers seeing themselves living in the property and for them paying top dollar.

A shabby or overgrown garden, greenery or lawn screams "poorly maintained" to buyers looking for move-in-ready homes. Let us prune long-neglected bushes and shrubs, remove weeds from lawns and pavements, and spruce up window and flower boxes.

Exterior home staging makes the property look newer, well cared for and more welcoming. We'll create an inviting experience to inspire buyers and generate a positive mood before they ever set foot through the front door.
