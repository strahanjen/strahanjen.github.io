About

Dedicated to creating your dream garden.

My Garden Goddess is a Twin Cities garden design company. We believe your garden is a place where you celebrate life. We have a passion for the great outdoors. For sustainable resources. For native plants. And for designing outdoor moments tailored to your personality.

Suzanne Gilbert, owner and Chief Garden Goddess, brings more than 20 years of visual design experience to her unique approach to garden design. Suzanne works one-on-one with you to create gardens that inspire, calm, delight and express your personality. Her personal design philosophy: to honor nature and the joy of living things while helping clients express their personality and design sensibility through their gardens.

Our full-service, sustainable garden design, build and installation firm serves homeowners, realtors and commercial clients in and around the Twin Cities metro area. If you are looking for front yard, back yard or full property gardening and landscaping; cleanup, staging or maintenance services; colorful window boxes or containers; or party or holiday planters and décor, we're here to help.
