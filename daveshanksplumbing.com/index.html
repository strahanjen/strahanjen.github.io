About Dave Shanks Plumbing

Recently relocated to Folsom, CA from the San Francisco Bay Area, Dave Shanks Plumbing has over 35 years of experience in residential remodels, new contruction and commercial work.

We work quickly and carefully, protecting your space with dropcloths as we work. We are tidy and always leave your space as we found it.

As a longstanding member of the Plumbing-Heating-Cooling Contractors Association of California, we take advantage of continuing education and professional development opportunities. You can be confident that we are well-versed on the lastest plumbing code and best practices.
